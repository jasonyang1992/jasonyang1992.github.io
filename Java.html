Black Jack

BlackJack, is a game where the player is verse the dealer. You start with two cards between 2 to 11. The objective is to sum your cards to 21 or have your cards sum higher than the dealer. You can keep hitting to increase your sum however if you pass 21, the dealer will win. The dealer can only go up to 17, so use that your advantage however if your sum of cards in equal to the dealer. The dealer will still win due to house rules.
